About
The Story Behind
the Stories
Portrait

Cameron Hood is a feature animation director, story artist, and creative leader with over three decades of experience shaping Hollywood's most iconic animated worlds. His career spans the golden eras of both hand-drawn and CG animation — from Warner Bros. to DreamWorks to Illumination.

As Co-Director and Head of Story on Megamind, Cameron helped create a film that grossed $321 million worldwide and launched a franchise. His storyboard work has contributed to films with a combined global box office exceeding $2 billion.

Every frame is a chance to make someone feel something they've never felt before. That's the power of visual storytelling.

Trained in the lineage of Chuck Jones and classical animation masters, Cameron bridges old-school craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. He led DreamWorks' historic transition from 2D to 3D production — equally fluent in hand-drawn emotion and CG spectacle.

Beyond the studio, Cameron hosted an internationally acclaimed Character Animation Lecture Series and traveled with DreamWorks CEO Jeffrey Katzenberg on the studio's university outreach program, inspiring the next generation of animators.
